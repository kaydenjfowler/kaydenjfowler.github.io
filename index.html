About Me

I'm Kayden, a 13 year old passionate developer from New Zealand.

I've mainly worked around Arduino C++ but have branched into other development areas. I now work with many languages, and cover aspects such as Application Development & Web Development.

I have always had an interest in designing programs and how things worked. So back when I started messing with computers, I got really intrigued into how computers worked and how they operated. This is where I discovered that they ran on code, so I began to learn scratch making simple games and then onto more complicated programming languages such as HTML, Arduino C++. Etc.

This is where my interest for programming sparked as I began to learn more and more about programing and also about computer development. From this point I moved onto other languages to take my interest further and build other things. I now work on projects such as this website you are reading, using a range of HTML & CSS.

So now I continue to expand my knowledge and interest by continuing to learn new languages, and also making new projects that benefit our world.
